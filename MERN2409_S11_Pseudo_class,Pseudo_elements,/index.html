Rohit Sharma

  • Born: April 30, 1987, Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra
  • Age: 37y
  • Batting Style: Right hand Bat
  • Bowling Style: Right Offbreak
  • Playing Role: Top order Batter

Rohit Sharma holds the record for the most double centuries in ODIs, with three, including a world-record 264 runs.As captain, Rohit Sharma has led Mumbai Indians to a record five IPL titles.He was part of India's 2007 T20 World Cup and 2013 Champions Trophy-winning teams. Rohit scored a record five centuries in the 2019 ICC World Cup, finishing as the tournament's top scorer.He became India's full-time captain across all formats in 2022.

Virat Kohli

  • Born: November 05, 1988, Delhi
  • Age:35y
  • Batting Style: Right hand Bat
  • Bowling Style: Right arm Medium
  • Playing Role: Top order Batter

Virat Kohli, a modern cricket legend, has achieved remarkable milestones in his career. He led India to their first-ever Test series victory in Australia in 2018-19 and became the fastest player to reach 8,000, 9,000, and 10,000 ODI runs. Kohli is the only player to win the prestigious ICC Cricketer of the Year award three times, including back-to-back wins in 2017 and 2018. He played a key role in India’s 2011 World Cup triumph and reached 70 international centuries faster than anyone before him. His leadership and consistency define his legacy as one of the all-time greats.

Jashprith Bhumrah

  • Born: December 06, 1993, Ahmedabad
  • Age: 30y
  • Batting Style: Right hand Bat
  • Bowling Style: Right arm Fast
  • Playing Role: Bowler

Jasprit Bumrah, one of India’s premier fast bowlers, has redefined pace bowling with his unique action and lethal accuracy. Known for his deadly yorkers and ability to bowl in the death overs, he played a pivotal role in India’s series wins across formats. Bumrah became the first Asian bowler to take a five-wicket haul in South Africa, England, and Australia during the same calendar year in 2018. He was instrumental in India’s historic Test series win in Australia and has been a key figure in the Indian Premier League (IPL), helping Mumbai Indians secure multiple titles.

Surya Kumar Yadav

  • Born: September 14, 1990, Mumbai, Maharashtra
  • Age: 34y
  • Batting Style: Right hand Bat
  • Bowling Style: Right arm Medium, Right arm Offbreak
  • Playing Role: Top order Batter

Suryakumar Yadav, often hailed as "SKY," has quickly risen as one of India's most dynamic and versatile cricketers. Known for his explosive batting and innovative shot-making, he has been a vital player in India’s limited-overs setup. After consistent performances in the Indian Premier League (IPL) with Mumbai Indians, where he played crucial roles in their title-winning campaigns, Suryakumar made a sensational debut for India in 2021, scoring a half-century in his first international innings. His ability to dominate bowlers all around the ground, especially in T20s, has earned him a reputation as one of the most exciting talents in modern cricket.
